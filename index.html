Executive Summary

Vector Bellows deploys high-density sovereign AI compute infrastructure using proprietary 3D-printed geopolymer technology in subterranean environments. Each node is hardened against electromagnetic interference, operates on closed-loop geothermal cooling (zero freshwater), and provides military-grade redundancy for mission-critical workloads.

Our Phase 1 deployment—18,000 nodes across distributed underground sites—targets 6,000 MW of operational capacity within 36 months. This infrastructure serves as the backbone for sovereign AI applications, edge compute networks, and cryptographic operations requiring extreme resilience.

We are raising capital in two tiers: Sovereign Partners ($1M+ commitment, board-level access) and Common Pool (waterfall distribution). All capital is deployed via step-up tranches tied to construction milestones and permitting clearance.
